About PGS

PGS provides Extensive and Modular Process-Automation solutions for the Oil & Gas, petrochemical and power & energy industries. We offer state-of-the-art integrated solutions in compliance with international standards while meeting clients’ expectations.

We are committed to be a leading-Expert in providing Process & Automation solutions worldwide. We aim to be the obvious selection of our clients, our collaborators and our partners.
